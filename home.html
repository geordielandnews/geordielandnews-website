Founded in 2022, Geordie Land News is a fan-built Newcastle United brand that brings Magpies supporters together across our website, Twitter, and Instagram. What started as a small passion project has grown into a lively space where fans can get quick updates, honest opinions, and dedicated coverage of everything happening at NUFC. Geordie Land News is all about celebrating the club, connecting supporters, and giving fans a voice in the heartbeat of Newcastle United.
